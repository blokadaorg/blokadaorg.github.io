Siyakwamukela futhi ngiyabonga ngokusebenzisa i-Blokada DNS!

I-Blokada ikhululekile futhi ivulekile futhi ihlonipha ubumfihlo bakho. Ngokusebenzisa uvumelana nemigomo yesevisi nenqubomgomo yobumfihlo, ungathola thola lapha.

Sithanda ukukusiza ngezinyathelo zakho zokuqala:

  1. Amaseva ejwayelekile we-DNS asetshenziswe njengamanje. Ungayishintsha ngokuthinta i-icon Isithonjana seDNS.
  2. I-Blokada isethwe ukuqala lapho ifoni yakho iqalwa. Ungavula uma uvula Qala ku-boot.
  3. I-Blokada izobe isebenza kuphela uma uxhumekile ku-intanethi. Ungayishintsha ngokubeka i- ku-intanethi kuphela ku off.
  4. Ku Ezivunyelwe ungachaza ukuthi yiziphi izinhlelo zokusebenza ezingafanele zisebenzise amaseva wakho we-DNS. Bazosebenzisa amaseva we-DNS okuzenzakalelayo kunalokho.
  5. Ukuthinta ekhoneni eliphezulu ngakwesokudla ungathola okunye okukhethwa kukho kanye nemenyu Usizo lapho ungathola ulwazi oluthe xaxa mayelana nezinketho ezahlukene eBlokada.
