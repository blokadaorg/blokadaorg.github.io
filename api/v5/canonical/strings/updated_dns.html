Blokada Slim v4.4

Your Blokada just got updated!

There are two editions of Blokada. The one you are using, available on Google Play, is called Blokada Slim (old name: DNS changer by Blokada). This is not an adblocker. It gives you a possibility to change your DNS servers, and also to use our brand new VPN service (optional). The open source version of Blokada is available at our website.

You may have noticed the new UI in this update. This is the new v4 interface, and we hope you will like it! For more info about the changes check our changelog.
