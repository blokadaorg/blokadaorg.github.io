I-Blokada, ivimba izikhangiso

Uma ufuna ukuvimba kahle izikhangiso, ama-tracker, i-malware, onga ohlelweni lwedatha yakho, ukusheshisisa idivayisi yakho futhi uvikele ubumfihlo bakho ngohlelo lokusebenza olulodwa, lapho-ke iBlokada ingeyakho. Imahhala, iphephile futhi umthombo ovulekile.

Blokada works across all of your apps, not just your browser! It doesn't require root or jailbreak, supports both Wi-Fi and mobile networks, and is perfectly safe and simple to use. Vele uyivule, yona izosebenza ngokuthula!

Indaba yethu

I-Blokada yaqala ukukhishwa ngoLwezi lwa-2016 ngu-Karol Gusak unjiniyela. Umgomo kwakuwukugcina amadivaysi akhululekile futhi agweme okuqukethiwe okungadingeki okusebenzisa idatha yeselula ngokweqile. Izici zokuqala nezilungiso zithunyelwe masinyane, isihloko sohlelo kwiforamu elidumile le-XDA sasinezibuyekezo zehora. Ukuze itholakale kalula kubasebenzisi futhi ibasize kalula, iphrojekthi yavula indlela youxhumana kwi-Telegram. Amalunga amabili okuqala kwakunguKarol kanye nelinye ilunga lesigungu, uPéter Róth. Kamuva waqala ukuvuselela ikhasi le-Facebook ne-website yevimba sikhangiso, ngaphandle kwalokho waze wasiza ekamelweni lengxoxo elikhula ngokushesha.

Ukwakha iqembu eliza ukuhumusha uhlelo, lakhe okuqukethiwe, ukuhlola nokuguqula izinguqulo ze-beta nokuphatha amakhasi omphakathi, I-Blokada Insiders yasungulwa ngo-2018 futhi isebenza ngenkuthalo ukwenza i-Blokada ibe impumelelo eyiyona. Did you know today Blokada is available in 25 languages?
USebastian Januszevski ujoyine ukuphathwa kwesiteshi seTelegram, esiza amalungu futhi wabamba iqhaza ekuhlolweni kohlelo, enza kabusha imibiko yabasebenzisi. Ngokusekelwe kokukhathazeka kwakhe nolwazi, i-Blokada yaqina futhi yithembekile.

UKarol wazalwa e-Poland. Ngemva kokuchitha iminyaka eminingana eSweden, usebuyele eWarsaw, lapho ehlanganyela khona emisebenzini ehlukahlukene yeselula njengomthuthukisi. Awumangele, angithi?
UPéter uvela eHungary, esiseduze nenhloko-dolobha yezwe, iBudapest, lapho asebenza njengonjiniyela emkhakheni we-IT, ekwazi ukujula kobuchwepheshe be-Big Data, esekela izinkampani emhlabeni wonke ezaziwayo ukuze zifeze umgomo wazo.
USebastian uhlala futhi usebenza eBuenos Aires lapho efundisa khona izibalo nokulungiswa kwedatha eyunivesithi. Ungumthuthukisi wokuzilibazisa ku-PHP ne-JavaScript, futhi kukhishwe ama-plugin ambalwa we-WordPress ngaphansi kwegama lakhe.

Kusukela ekuqaleni, i-Blokada ilayishwe futhi ifakwe kumadivayisi angaphezu kuka-200.000, kusukela kumafoni kuya kumathebhulethi nakuma-smart TV.

Sithini isibonakalo sethu? Ithimba lizosebenza ukusekela ububanzi bamadivayisi, lenze kube lula ukuxhuma kunsizakalo yangempela ye-VPN ngenkathi i-Blokada isebenza futhi ivikela ubumfihlo bakho ngaphezulu, ikunike ulwazi oluningi mayelana nezikhangiso ozivikelwe kuzo.

I-Blokada yenziwe nguwe

Noma kunjalo, ukuthuthukisa uhlelo lwekhwalithi njengeBlokada kudinga inani elikhulu lemithombo. Le phrojekthi ikhona ngenxa yokusekelwa kwabantu abafana nawe!
